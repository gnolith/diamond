# Code of conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project for everyone.
Participants must communicate constructively, respect differing experience and
viewpoints, accept responsibility for mistakes, and focus criticism on ideas
and code rather than people.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, unwanted sexual attention,
doxxing, and sustained disruption are unacceptable. Maintainers may edit or
remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation
when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Reports will be
handled confidentially, promptly, and without retaliation. Before a public
release this policy will be replaced or expanded with named enforcement
contacts and a published escalation process.
